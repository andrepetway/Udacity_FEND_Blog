Orsa & Winston


Author: Andre Petway - May 6, 2024

Location: 122 W 4th Street, Los Angeles, CA 90013
Hours: Tues - Sat. 5PM - 10PM
Menu Options:

  1. Prix Fixe: 5-Course Tasting Menu

  2. Omakase: $125 per person

  3. Vegetarian: $135 per person



Mouth-watering. Dazzling. Succulent. Grandiose.

Chef Josef Centeno should be very proud of the work he and his crew put together each night for Orsa and Winston. Nestled on the border between Skid Row and Little Tokyo is an unassuming door leading to an intimate table space overseeing a skillful kitchen. Flavor pairings are unlike anything I had ever had before. Seafood is the protein throughout the courses. We opted for the 5-course meal on the occasion of our anniversary. I am not sure if the occasion had anything to do with the service, but in the end we received 4 additional courses for a total of 9. Chef started us with a dashi based custard topped with caviar and the texture and warmth tasted great on the palate. It felt like starting with a savory dessert.
